# Recipe Shelf Privacy Policy Last Updated: May 30, 2026 Recipe Shelf respects your privacy. ## Information We Collect Recipe Shelf is designed to help you store and organize recipes, photos, ingredients, cooking notes, and related information. The information you add to Recipe Shelf may include: * Recipe titles * Ingredients * Directions * Notes * Recipe photos * Cooking history * Categories and favorites This information is stored locally on your device and may be synchronized through Apple's iCloud services if iCloud Sync is enabled. ## Photos Recipe Shelf may request access to your photo library so you can attach photos to recipes. Photos are only accessed when you choose to select them. Recipe Shelf does not upload your photos to any servers operated by the developer. ## iCloud Sync If enabled, Recipe Shelf uses Apple's CloudKit service to synchronize your data across devices signed into the same Apple ID. Data synchronized through CloudKit is subject to Apple's privacy and security practices. ## OCR Processing Recipe Shelf may use Apple's Vision framework to extract text from recipe photos. OCR processing occurs on your device. Recipe images and extracted text are not sent to developer-operated servers. ## Backup and Export Recipe Shelf allows you to export backups of your recipe collection. Exported files are stored in locations you choose, such as the Files app, iCloud Drive, or other supported storage providers. You are responsible for protecting exported backup files. ## Analytics Recipe Shelf does not collect personal analytics or usage tracking information. ## Advertising Recipe Shelf does not display third-party advertising. ## Third-Party Services Recipe Shelf relies on Apple services including: * CloudKit * iCloud * Photos * Vision These services are governed by Apple's terms and privacy policies. ## Data Retention Your recipe data remains under your control. You may delete recipes, photos, cooking history, or the application at any time. ## Children's Privacy Recipe Shelf is not directed toward children under the age of 13. ## Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact: [support@keystonechms.com](mailto:support@keystonechms.com) ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
